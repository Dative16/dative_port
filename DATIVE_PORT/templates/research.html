AI in Healthcare Diagnostics: Machine Learning Approaches for Disease Detection
Authors Datius Daud, Dr. Benstone, Dr. Adam Kilungu
Publication Date January 15, 2024
Journal Journal of Medical AI
Volume/Issue Vol. 12, Issue 3
DOI 10.1016/j.jmai.2024.01.015
Pages 215-230
Abstract

This paper explores machine learning approaches for early disease detection in resource-limited settings. We present a comprehensive analysis of various ML algorithms applied to medical diagnostics, with a focus on implementations that can function effectively with limited computational resources. Our research includes case studies on malaria detection, tuberculosis screening, and early diabetes prediction, demonstrating how AI can augment traditional diagnostic methods while reducing costs and improving accessibility. The paper also discusses the challenges of implementing AI solutions in low-resource settings and proposes frameworks for overcoming these barriers.

Keywords

Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Healthcare Diagnostics, Disease Detection, Resource-Limited Settings, Medical Imaging

Sentiment Analysis in Financial Services: Evaluating Customer Satisfaction through NLP
Authors Datius Daud, Rashidi M., Prof. Johnson
Publication Date March 22, 2024
Journal Financial Technology Journal
Volume/Issue Vol. 8, Issue 2
DOI 10.1016/j.fintech.2024.02.008
Pages 145-162
Abstract

This study examines the application of natural language processing techniques for sentiment analysis in financial services, specifically focusing on customer feedback for banking applications and financial service platforms. We developed and compared multiple NLP models for analyzing customer reviews from app stores, social media, and support channels. Our research demonstrates how sentiment analysis can provide actionable insights for improving customer experience, identifying pain points in digital banking services, and predicting customer churn. The paper also introduces a novel approach for handling financial jargon and context-specific language in sentiment classification.

Keywords

Sentiment Analysis, Natural Language Processing, Financial Services, Customer Satisfaction, FinTech, Digital Banking
